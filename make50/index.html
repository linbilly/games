Menu & Rules

Goal: Combine all the tiles to reach the target number!

  • Tap a number, tap an operator (+, −, ×, ÷), then tap another number to merge them.
  • Double-tap or click Undo to undo the merge.
  • Skip a question you are stuck on, but it breaks a combo and -200 points. If <0 points, you lost the game!
  • Hint shows you the final step. It will break your combo but doesn't incur other penalty. Use once per game.

How to Play

Goal: Combine all the tiles to reach the target number!

  • Tap a number, tap an operator (+, −, ×, ÷), then tap another number to merge them.
  • Double-tap a combined tile to break it apart and undo the move.
  • Clear the board by combining all starting tiles into a single shiny gold tile!

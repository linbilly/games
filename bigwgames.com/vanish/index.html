How to Play: The Invisible Board

The game is based on Gomoku (also known as Five in a Row), but introduces a hardcore memory element that completely changes your strategy.

  • The Goal: Be the first player to get an unbroken row of five pieces horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  • The Twist: After a piece is placed, it has a strict countdown (e.g., 10 seconds). When time is up, the piece turns completely invisible.
  • The Trap: Invisible pieces are still on the board! If you try to place a new piece on an occupied square, you commit a "Mistake."
  • The Penalty: Committing a mistake briefly reveals the entire board to both players and adds a strike to your penalty meter. Collect 3 strikes, and you forfeit your turn.
  • The Strategy: You must memorize the board state. Track your hidden lines to build an unstoppable Connect 5, and try to force your opponent into clicking your invisible pieces!
  • The Rules: Choose 'Standard' play for a casual Gomoku game without forbidden moves. Choose 'Renju' to forbid 3-3, 4-4, or overlines for the first player. Choose the tournament-style 'Swap2' rule for ultimate complexity and balance.

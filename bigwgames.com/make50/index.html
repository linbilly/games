How to Play

Goal: Combine all the tiles on the board to reach the target number (10, 20, or 50 depending on your difficulty level).

  • Tap a number, tap an operator (+, ‚àí, √ó, √∑), then tap another number to merge them.

  • Clear the board by combining all starting tiles into a single shiny gold tile!
  • Earn higher scores by solving puzzles quickly!

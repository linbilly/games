How to play

  • Move with ← and →, jump with Space (or use the on-screen buttons).
  • Stand near a hole to open the brick choices.
  • Pick the brick that completes the target (5 in Level 1, 10 in Level 2).
Keyboard: ← → Space • Touch: on-screen buttons
